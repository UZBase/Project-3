We recommend scheduling an appointment before you drop in, since that’s the best way to ensure that you get right in without waiting – but we certainly welcome walk-ins if you happen to be in the area on other business. There might be a small delay, but we’ll certainly do our best to get you in promptly even if you haven’t made an appointment prior to stopping by. Of course, you’ll probably want to schedule some extra time in your day when you come to visit us, so that you can take advantage of all the wonderful sights and sounds that the Garment District has to offer.

We’re located on the 2nd Floor of 1006 6th Avenue in New York City – in an area of town that’s just filled to the brim with exciting places to see and things to do. There are a seemingly endless series of adventures to be had from our location inside the heart of the Garment District. So, while you’re in the area to get your eyelash extensions, you can also take in some of the city’s most remarkable and famous landmarks.

We’re within a block of Macy’s, so there are certainly opportunities for shopping and other activities while you’re here. 34th Street Penn Station is less than two blocks away too – with Herald Square and Madison Square Garden nearly as close. A little farther in the distance, you can see the Empire State Building – once the tallest building in the world. If you head north from our salon, you can see Times Square – one of the most famous landmarks on the planet. Throughout the area, you can find many places to eat, shop, take in the sights, or just enjoy the sounds of the city!

Inside the salon, you’ll find a haven away from the troubles of the world. Here, you’ll receive nothing but the care and attention you need as our professional technicians – each licensed by the State of New York – lovingly apply your eyelash extensions. Our commitment to your lash works experience ensures that we work with you to find the best lash choice to accentuate your beautiful features, and then apply those lashes using our custom application techniques.

We have long been leaders in the eyelash extension community in New York City, and that leadership shines through in the work we do each day.
